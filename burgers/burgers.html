Burgers

Classic american dish

Perfect for cookouts

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Beef
  • potatos
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • sauce
  1. Season beef, form pattys
  2. Wash potatos chop in quarters put in airfryer 20 minutes 420
  3. Put pattys on grill cook till down
  4. Add cheese while finishing cooking
  5. Put buns on grill to brown
  6. combine with condiments you want

Enjoy!

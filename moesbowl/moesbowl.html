Moes Bowl

The combination of chicken rice and greens is a staple in most diets

This moes bowl knock off contains high protein, with good carbs and fats

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigs
  • White Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  1. Season and cook chicken
  2. While chicken is cooking preheat rice
  3. Combine after both have finished cooking
  4. Add cheese while still hot to melt

Enjoy!

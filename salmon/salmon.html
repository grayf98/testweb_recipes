Salmon

Healthy Fish high in Protein

Filled with healthy fatty acids, salmon is a classic protein.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • White Rice
  • Chickpeas
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  1. cover salmon in dijon mustard and season with old bay
  2. Throw salmon in air fryer for 20 minutes at 420 F
  3. Cook chickpeas and rice
  4. Put it all together

Enjoy!
